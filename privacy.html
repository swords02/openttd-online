Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-07

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use openttdonline.com (“the Site”). The Site is a community fan project that provides a browser-playable version of OpenTTD.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information by default. The Site does not require an account or login and does not track users unless privacy-friendly analytics are enabled.

2.1 Local Storage

The game may store configuration files, save files, and preferences in your browser’s local storage or IndexedDB. This data remains on your device unless you choose to export or clear it.

2.2 Optional Analytics (If Enabled)

If privacy-focused analytics (e.g., Plausible or Umami) are enabled, the Site may collect aggregated, non-personal data such as:

  • Pages visited and approximate time spent
  • Browser/OS type
  • Approximate region (no precise location)
  • Referring website

This data does not identify individual users.

3. Cookies

The Site does not use cookies unless analytics or third-party services are added. Any future changes will be documented in this policy.

4. Save Files & Uploaded Data

When you import a save file to play in the browser:

  • The file is processed locally within your browser.
  • No save files are uploaded to the server by default.
  • No game content is transmitted to us unless explicitly documented for a specific feature.

5. Third-Party Links

The Site may link to external resources (e.g., OpenTTD.org, mod repositories). Those sites have their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Revisions will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact
